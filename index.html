Our Story

A love that bloomed.

Our story: You don't know when love hits you—you just wake up one day thinking about nothing but holding her hand and keeping her safe.

That's exactly what happened to us. Fate brought us together, and from our very first conversation, we realized we were meant for each other—like we were sent to find and heal one another.

How We Met

The beginning

At work place, the first time I really saw her, everything just stopped—she was the most beautiful person I had ever seen. I gathered the courage, walked up, and said, "Hi, konjo." After that, the conversation just flowed like we'd known each other forever.

First Date

A night to remember

In a small coffee shop, we shared soft cake—she had a Sprite, and I had just still water. Our conversation was sincere and intimate, and in that moment, we realized we were meant for one another. From then on, I've been holding her hand—and I'll keep holding it forever.

The Proposal

Yes, forever

In the place I had always dreamed of proposing—right in front of the one who gifted me my whole world, Entoto Kidane Mihret Church—I asked the most important question of my life. It was a day filled with emotion, love, and more tears than I expected (the good kind).
